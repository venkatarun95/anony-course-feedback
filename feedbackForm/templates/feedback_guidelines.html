Feedback Guidelines

Feedback Guidelines

Hope you are enjoying your courses and are having an excellent learning experience. All efforts are being made to make the course interesting and useful.

Thank you for taking time out to fill this form. Your feedback here is very valuable as it will help improve the teaching standards in the institute and aid future batches.

While filling the form, please keep in interests of the whole batch and try not to let your performance (which we hope was great) in the course affect your feedback.

Further, you can rest assured that no pain has been spared to ensure that this feedback is anonymous. Only with your password (which is not stored in the server) can anyone know which feedback was yours. Otherwise the information is totally lost. Of course, you must not mention any details that can identify you such as your name, roll number etc.

Instructions

How to Use

To your right you see a list of your courses along with your professors. The home page also shows the courses along with the percentage of the form that you have already filled for the various courses. Click on the course and fill in the feedback form.

Private Messaging

This portal provides you with the unique facility to communicate anonymously and securely with your instructor regarding any of your concerns. It is strongly recommended that you talk face-to-face, however in case you are hesitant to do do, you can use this platform. Rest assured that you identity is not revealed unless you yourself so so through the messages and that nobody other than your instructor (and yourself) can view it.
