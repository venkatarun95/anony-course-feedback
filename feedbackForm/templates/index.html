Feedback Guidelines

> Give specific suggestions as to how the course could be improved.

> Give genuine and constructive feedbacks, it helps alot in improvement.
> Try not to let your performance (which we hope was great) in the course affect your feedback.
> Of course, you must not mention any details that can identify you such as your name, roll number etc.
> Although your personal identification is not linked to your responses, course evaluations are important,formal documents.
